About Me

Economist with strong skills in research, academic writing, econometric analysis, and data management. I have experience in teaching, have been an author and co-author of academic publications, and am the coordinator of the research group "Rethinking Development" (REDE). I stand out academically and professionally for my meticulousness and leadership. I am a curious person, passionate about teaching, and my professional goal is to contribute positively to the well-being of young people and other vulnerable populations in our country through my work as an economist.
